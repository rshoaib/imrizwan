### "Excluded by 'noindex' tag" — 2 URLs (investigate)

Two pages on the site are sending `<meta name="robots" content="noindex">` or an `X-Robots-Tag` header. Nothing in `app/blog/[slug]/page.tsx` adds noindex, so the source is likely either `/privacy-policy`, a tag/category landing page, or a draft flag in frontmatter. **Drill into Indexing → Pages → "Excluded by 'noindex' tag" in GSC and confirm the two URLs aren't real blog posts.**

### Tag landing page is thin

`/blog?tag=sharepoint` was crawled-not-indexed. Tag pages without real curation copy are classic thin content. Either remove tag links from sitemap (current behavior, since they're query-string), or add a one-paragraph intro and a curated featured-post list above the auto list — then index them deliberately.
